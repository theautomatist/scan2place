scan2place

Scan the LCSC/JLCPCB packaging QR codes with your phone and instantly check off the matching component in your KiCad iBOM on the PC — no searching, no comparing.

  1. Upload an iBOM with the button in the sidebar, or pick one from the list.
  2. Open it on the PC — it opens as the Viewer.
  3. Click Connect scanner (top right) and scan the QR code it shows with your phone.
  4. Scan a part's packaging → it lights up and gets ticked off in the BOM.
